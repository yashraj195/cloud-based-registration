WHY WE EXIST

Three things we're stubborn about

Learn out loud

Every session is built around explaining, not just absorbing. If you can teach it back to the group, you actually know it.

Ship real things

No toy exercises for their own sake. Sprints end with a working demo, a pushed repo, or a solved problem set — something you can point to.

Show up for each other

Miss a class, get the notes. Stuck at midnight, someone answers. It's a group chat that actually replies — not a silent Discord server.
